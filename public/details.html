< arkanoid >

Arkanoid is a classic viseo game where players control a paddle to bounce a ball and break bricks arranged in various patterns on the screen. In the present implementation it is also possible to change the orientstion of the game field.In the smartphone version only horizontal is aviable.

Here are some of the game's featues:

Objective

The ofjective of <Arkanoid> is to clear all the bricks on the screen without letting the ball off the paddle

Game mechanics

Players use the paddle to maneuver the ball and hit the bricks.
As the player breaks the bricks, power-ups and bonuses appear that can be collected for additional points or benefits

Levels

The game features multiple levels with increasing difficulty as players progress through each one. Each level has a different layout of bricks

Bricks

The bricks are arrenged in various patterns and colors that require different numbers of hits before they break.
The configuration of the bricks can be generated automatically or configured independently.

Progression

As players progress through the game's levels, the patterns of bricks gradually become more complex and challenging, and the speed of the ball increases
